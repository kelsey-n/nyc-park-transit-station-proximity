Proximity to Parks & Transit Stations in NYC

Data

This map was created using Neighborhood Indicators downloaded from NYU Furman Center's data hub, CoreData.nyc . At the Community District level, this map shows:

Park Proximity - The percentage of residential units that are within a quarter mile of a park

Transit Station Proximity - The percentage of residential units that are within a half mile walk of a station entrance for the New York City Subway, Long Island Rail Road, PATH, Amtrak, Metro-North Railroad, or Staten Island Railway

Click on the buttons below to show, hide and layer the data on the map. Then hover over each district on the map to see its name and exact percentage.
