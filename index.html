Proximity to Parks & Transit Stations in NYC

Data

This map was created using Neighborhood Indicators downloaded from NYU Furman Center's data hub, CoreData.nyc . At the Community District level, this map shows:

Park Proximity
The percentage of residential units that are within a quarter mile of a park

Transit Station Proximity
The percentage of residential units that are within a half mile walk of a station entrance for the New York City Subway, Long Island Rail Road, PATH, Amtrak, Metro-North Railroad, or Staten Island Railway



How to use this map

Click the buttons on the map to show the data distributions either individually or both layered at once. Then hover over each district on the map to see its name and exact numbers.
